## Objectives

- Match Creative Commons and Open Data Commons licenses to reuse goals
- Distinguish dataset licenses from companion software licenses (MIT / Apache / BSD / GPL)
- Explain why sensitive health or community-controlled data need restricted access
- Describe the openness-versus-privacy trade-off for license choice
- Recognize ethical risks of reinforcing bias with open datasets
- State why communities should control how their data are shared
