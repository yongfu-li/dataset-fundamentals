Chapter 5 — Data Cleaning and Preprocessing

Follow the path in order: read the bridge, watch the part, then take the quiz before moving on. Downloads are available if you prefer to study offline.

What you will learn

  • Distinguish data cleaning from preprocessing and state why both precede modeling
  • Classify missing-data mechanisms (MCAR, MAR, MNAR) and common defect types
  • Apply deletion, imputation, deduplication, standardization, and outlier handling
  • Scale, encode, bin, engineer, and transform features for learning algorithms
  • Use pandas, scikit-learn, and related tools in reproducible workflows
  • Follow best practices for documentation, leakage avoidance, and class imbalance
  • Connect cleaning pipelines to retail and healthcare case studies
  • Recognize advanced directions: Bayesian imputation, streaming prep, and explainability
