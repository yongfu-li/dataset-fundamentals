Chapter 4 — Data Annotation

Follow the path in order: read the bridge, watch the part, then take the quiz before moving on. Downloads are available if you prefer to study offline.

What you will learn

  • Define data annotation and explain why label quality bounds supervised learning
  • Match annotation techniques to text, image, audio, video, and tabular modalities
  • Compare manual, semi-automated, automated, and crowdsourced annotation workflows
  • Select open-source and commercial annotation tools for modality and scale
  • Apply quality control through guidelines, inter-annotator agreement, and gold-set audits
  • Anticipate scale, bias, ethics, and rare-class challenges in annotation programs
  • Recognize annotation patterns in autonomous driving, healthcare, and e-commerce
  • Follow best-practice habits for guidelines, audits, diversity, and pre-labeling
  • Situate emerging AI-assisted, synthetic, and real-time labeling against QC gates

Learning path

Before part 1 Chapters 2 and 3 covered how records enter a dataset and what obligations attach to them. This chapter asks how raw records become supervised training signal. This first part defines annotation, shows why label quality often caps model accuracy, and previews labeled examples across text, image, audio, and video.
