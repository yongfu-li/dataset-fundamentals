Chapter 6 — Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Follow the path in order: read the bridge, watch the part, then take the quiz before moving on. Downloads are available if you prefer to study offline.

What you will learn

  • Define EDA as exploration before formal modeling and link it to Chapter 5 cleaning
  • Classify variable types and compute central tendency, dispersion, and shape summaries
  • Choose univariate, bivariate, and multivariate visualizations for EDA goals
  • Detect missing values, outliers, duplicates, inconsistency, and imbalance during exploration
  • Follow a four-step EDA workflow with pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, and related tools
  • Apply correlation analysis, PCA, and other advanced exploration techniques
  • Practice reproducible, stakeholder-ready visualization and avoid common EDA pitfalls
  • Connect EDA patterns to retail, healthcare, fraud, and climate case studies
