Chapter 12 — Scalable Data Management

Follow the path in order: read the bridge, watch the part, then take the quiz before moving on. Downloads are available if you prefer to study offline.

What you will learn

  • Define scalable data management and explain elasticity, efficiency, and resilience
  • Compare databases, object storage, and lakehouse formats for large datasets
  • Describe distributed systems patterns including HDFS, Cassandra, and Kafka
  • Apply CAP theorem, sharding, and consistency models to storage design choices
  • Use dataset versioning tools such as DVC, Pachyderm, Delta Lake, and Iceberg at scale
  • Explain data lineage techniques, tools, benefits, and cross-platform gaps
  • Recognize scalable architectures in streaming media, analytics, and healthcare imaging
  • Identify emerging trends including serverless compute, graph stores, and streaming platforms
